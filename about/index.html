About Me

The Virtual Executive Assistant your business has been waiting for

I started InSyncEA because I saw a gap, brilliant founders and executives spending their best hours on tasks that weren't moving their businesses forward. I became the person who changes that. Not just someone who handles tasks, but someone who genuinely cares about your goals, protects your time, and keeps everything running smoothly so you can lead without distraction.

What sets me apart is simple: I think ahead. Before you realise something needs doing, I've already done it. Before a problem surfaces, I've quietly solved it. My clients don't just get organised, they get back the mental clarity, focus, and energy that got them to where they are in the first place.

I work across calendar management, inbox management, travel logistics, CRM, ecommerce, bookkeeping, research, document creation, system set-up, and beyond. Whatever your business demands, I show up prepared, discreet, and fully invested, treating your business with the same care and intention I would give my own.

I keep my client roster deliberately small. Every person I work with gets my complete attention, my best thinking, and a partnership that truly makes a difference.
