Bringing Order to a Growing Brooklyn Brand

The Founder & CEO, Brooklyn Tea, Brooklyn NY

Brooklyn Tea had grown rapidly from a neighbourhood tea shop into a multi-channel brand with events, wholesale, and a busy retail presence. The founder was managing customer communications, vendor relationships, event logistics, and team scheduling simultaneously, with no system holding it together. Things were slipping, and the energy needed for growth was being consumed by daily operations.

The Challenge

No centralised operations system. Customer and vendor emails going unanswered for days. Events being planned reactively. The founder spending 70% of their time on admin rather than brand strategy.

What I Did

Built a full operations framework in Notion, took over all vendor and customer correspondence, created event planning templates and timelines, and established a weekly rhythm of briefings that kept the founder informed without being pulled into every detail.

Executive Support · Creative & Design Studio

Rescuing a Creative Director's Calendar

Creative Director, Elevate Studios, New York

A creative director at a growing design studio was spending her most valuable hours in back-to-back meetings, responding to client emails, and chasing internal deadlines. Her creative output, the core reason clients hired the studio, was suffering. She needed an Executive Assistant who could take complete ownership of her schedule and communications without needing constant guidance.

The Challenge

Calendar with zero protected creative time. 200+ unread emails. Client follow-ups being missed. No briefing system before important meetings. Three missed deadlines in two months.

What I Did

Audited and restructured the entire calendar, cutting 8 recurring meetings, establishing 3 protected creative blocks weekly, and implementing a morning briefing document delivered every day by 8am. Took over inbox management completely.

The information collected may include your name, email address, company name, job title, and any details you share in your message. I do not collect sensitive personal data.

2. How I Use Your Information

I use your personal information solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiry and communicate with you about potential or existing services

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, I process your personal data on the following legal bases: your consent (when you submit the contact form), the performance of a contract (when providing services), and compliance with legal obligations.

4. Data Retention

I retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected. Contact enquiries are retained for up to 12 months unless an ongoing service relationship exists. Client data is retained for the duration of our engagement and for a reasonable period thereafter as required by law or legitimate business purposes.

This website may use essential cookies to ensure basic functionality. If analytics tools are used, they collect anonymised data about how visitors use the site (pages visited, time spent, device type). 6. Data Security

I take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. All client engagements are covered by a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before work begins. 7. Third-Party Services

Under applicable data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information I hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me at [email protected]. I will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy
